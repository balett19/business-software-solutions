Turbotax For small business is a powerful tool that enables small business owners to keep their finances on track. It has a variety of features, including bank account connectivity, mileage tracking, import of receipts, and quarterly tax payments. It's also much cheaper than hiring a tax preparer. This is an important consideration for small businesses, as costs are usually one of the biggest obstacles to profitability. Turbotax's user-friendly interface makes it easy to prepare and file taxes and it helps small businesses take advantage of all the tax breaks and benefits available to them. However, Turbotax can be expensive. A single tax return can cost nearly $200, which can be a bit much for small businesses. Additionally, it costs $49 per state return. It may be difficult to justify spending hundreds of dollars to use the software for small businesses. As a result, small business owners are advised to keep a low budget and to avoid paying too much for this user-friendly software.
